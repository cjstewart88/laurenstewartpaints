About Lauren

Lauren Stewart is a native of Atlanta, Georgia. Her passion for painting began early in her youth; however, as she pursued a future in medicine, she had little time to dedicate herself to her artwork. After recently leaving her medical career with the birth of her son, she has revisited the joys of creating art once more.

With no formal training in painting, Lauren is completely self-taught. She works in acrylics on canvas. Lauren's art focuses primarily on the serenity of nature with a specific focus on beautiful, open fields and majestic skies.

She currently lives in Smyrna, Georgia with her loving husband, son, and dog, Rocko.
